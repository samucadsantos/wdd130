Espirito Santos / Serra

Located in the dynamic southeastern region of Brazil, Espírito Santo borders the Atlantic Ocean, offering a striking contrast between bustling island capitals and lush mountain terrains. Its capital, Vitória, is one of the oldest cities in Brazil, while the neighboring highlands, settled by European immigrants, are home to iconic peaks and parks.
